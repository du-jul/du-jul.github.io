Start of development


- term "artificial intelligence" was coined.

- "global problem solver" was created.

- Article "Computing Machinery and Intelligence" published by Alan Turing.

- Georgetown experiment demonstrated fully automatic translation of more than 60 sentences from Russian to English.

- the first vehicle in history to successfully conquer the lunar surface

Development continues


By the 1980s, AI was revived. John Hopfield and David Rumelhart popularized "deep learning" methods that allowed computers to learn from experience. Expert systems were also created. This systems imitated the decision-making process of a human expert.

The first serious steps


- The great chess player Gary Kasparov lost to the computer.

- speech recognition software was implemented.

- Kismet robot could recognize and display emotions of human.
